# Product Mission (Lite)

CopyDrafter is a social media content editor that helps content creators and marketers generate personalized, on-brand content by combining AI writing assistance with style preservation and iterative editing workflows.

CopyDrafter serves content creators and marketing professionals who need to scale content production while maintaining consistent brand voice. Unlike generic AI writing tools, CopyDrafter analyzes creator's existing content to generate detailed style profiles and provides a unified interface with persistent context management for increasingly personalized content generation.
